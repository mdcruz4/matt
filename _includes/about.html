About me

I am first an engineer, but I have a degree in Communication Design and a high degree of talent in fine art and creative media in general. I didn't realize my dream goal until after I had tried my hand at many things including: two degrees, multiple employment changes, and several engineering positions.

After all those things, I realized I just wanted to be the creative guy I was always meant to be. I wanted to make toys, comics, games, animation, music and more. And in this case, for the first iteration at least, I want them all to be tied together. Also, if they need some engineering ingenuity, why not incorporate that? I know what you're thinking: there isn't much here now. But I say things will change quickly. So sit back and enjoy. The only limits to how far I can take this are the extent of your and my imagination."
