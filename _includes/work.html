Prototype Models

Rebel Snowspeeder
This was a neat project to prove that you could make a quality toy, based on a popular toyline, but at less cost. Although the materials did cost less, I think I probably lost money through time here. The project took about 4 months to complete from initial measurements and conversions, to painting and fabricating the tow-cable mechanism.
Rebel Snowspeeder
Altogether it was a great project that taught me lessons to use on later projects. For instance it's better to use Metric measurements when scaling from smaller blueprints, because Standard measurements make things more complicated with fractional inches and not using base-ten systems.
Rebel Snowspeeder
The speeder includes a working canopy, and an "in progress" tow-cable. It is at "full size"-3-3/4" scale. I say full size because many large 3-3/4" scale vehicles are slightly scaled down for various reasons.

Create Ur Toy
Ever want to customize or recreate your favorite toys; but don't know how, have the means, or won't because it's just too expensive? Well now you don't have to worry about those things. Create Ur Toy takes care of those problems for you. Create Ur Toy gives you step-by-step instructions on how to make fully-pose-able and detailed action figures. It also gives you the tools and materials to make them. You can make your own toy line, or make replicas of an existing one.
Create Ur Toy
